How to find model weight on MakerWorld
1. On MakerWorld, hover over the model/profile you want to print.
2. A floating panel will pop up — find the Weight label (usually in grams).
3. Enter the number (only digits) below.
Instructions:
1. Look at the list of Print Profiles on the MakerWorld page.
2. Type the profile name OR its position (1 is the top profile).
3. Use negative numbers to count from the bottom (e.g., -1 is the very last one).
If you leave this blank, Profile 1 will be selected.
